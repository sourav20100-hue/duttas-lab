Our "In-House Only" Sample Integrity Policy

Patients often ask why we do not offer home sample collection. The answer lies in clinical science and our uncompromising commitment to accuracy. Blood samples begin to degrade the moment they are drawn. Exposure to temperature fluctuations, physical agitation, and transit delays during home collection can alter cellular structures and skew test results, particularly for sensitive tests like lipid profiles and autoimmune markers. To guarantee 100% sample integrity, we enforce a strict "In-House Only" policy. When you visit our wheelchair-accessible, ground-floor facility, your sample is processed immediately in our robotic infrastructure, ensuring the most accurate report possible.
